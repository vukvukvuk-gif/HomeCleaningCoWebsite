Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

Do you bring supplies and equipment?

Yes — we bring our own supplies and standard equipment. You don’t need to provide anything. If you prefer we use specific products (or avoid certain ingredients), just let us know in your quote request.

What are your hours?

We’re available 7am–8pm, Monday through Sunday.

What if I’m not satisfied?

Let us know within 24 hours and we’ll come back to re-clean the specific areas you mention.

Do I need to be home?

No — many clients provide access instructions and come back to a spotless home.

How do you price cleanings?

Pricing depends on bedrooms, bathrooms, and the home’s condition. Request a quote and we’ll confirm an accurate estimate quickly.

What’s your reschedule or cancellation policy?

If you need to reschedule, please give us as much notice as possible so we can adjust the schedule and serve everyone on time.
